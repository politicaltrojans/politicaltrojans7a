ABOUT US

COMPANY PROFILE

J & J Inter Continental Enterprises Inc., was established as a direct response to the expanding import/export markets within the region and trade opportunities within China and other Asia Pacific regions particularly Australia. With our established networks, J & J Inter Continental Enterprises Inc., has relationship with a number of major multinational manufacturing plants located in the Peoples Republic of China (PROC), throughout Asia, Europe, United States, and Australia. We work with companies who are ISO 9001, HACCP, FAMI, IQNET certified to ensure high quality products. This gives us an undeniable edge in maintaining excellent quality products at very competitive prices.

VISION

To build long-term relationships with clients on the foundation of trust and through good quality of products that we provide in a consistent and on-going basis.

MISSION

We aim to provide clients with highly efficient and effective sourcing platform for a wide-range of products, ranging from feed, food additives, veterinary health products, pharmaceuticals, foodstuffs and general merchandise to packaging.
